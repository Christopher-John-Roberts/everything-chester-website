What's On Chester


Here you can find out What’s On in Chester, Cheshire and the surrounding area. The “What’s On Chester” page is brought to you by UPtoday.

UPtoday is a Community Interest Company that is building a giant interactive noticeboard for the City of Chester. Uptoday’s map shows events that are happening in Chester and surrounding area - today, tomorrow, or any day you choose.

Register with UPtoday to log in start adding Chester events and venues.

The more people add their own listings, the more useful and interesting the map becomes . . . so get adding Chester events and tell all your friends!







Join UPtoday and help fill the map with local Chester events.

There is loads going on in our great City. The information is already out there – on individual websites, noticeboards, posters, flyers, lampposts and facebook pages. UPtoday want to bring it together in one accessible place - comprehensive, sorted by place and time, quick and easy to view – the essential details linking you to the full story.

If you like the idea UPtoday would love you to join in - add a couple of events that you know about and tell organisers about the map.
