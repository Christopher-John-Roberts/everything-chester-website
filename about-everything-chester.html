About Everything Chester


Everything Chester started as a Twitter feed in April 2017, promising "Quality Not Quantity Chester Tweets". It covers news, events and pictures of Chester Cheshire.

EverythingChester.co.uk is still in early development, but watch this space!

For now you can read the latest Chester news, browse Chester jobs and view real time Chester traffic and weather.

Like us on Facebook or follow us on Twitter to keep up to date with everything going on in Chester, Cheshire in the UK.

You can also keep updated with Everything Chester by subscribing to our RSS Feed

Feel free to submit Chester stories, Chester information, events or photos to everything.chester@gmail.com or tweeting @every_chester.

We also welcome feedback, suggested improvements and additional Chester topics you would like to see covered on everythingchester.co.uk, just fill out our contact form.

Bookmark us, and visit again in a few weeks when we are up and running.

We look forward to seeing you again then.

Everything Chester’s Founder – Christopher John Roberts BSc (Hons) MRes


Chris is Chester born and bred, appreciative of his CH postcode and a very proud Cestrian. He is a jack of all trades and master of bioinformatics.

Born in the Countess of Chester Hospital in 1987 Chris was quickly whisked a stone’s throw over the Cheshire Flintshire border to a Chester suburb called Saltney. Saltney is the only town in the UK divided down the middle by the England and Wales border. His primary school was literally on the Flintshire side of a road named “Boundary Lane”, so Welsh lessons where on the curriculum.

Growing up “going to town” always meant Chester, and only 2 miles to the historic walls and city centre he often walked.



Chris achieved a first-class degree in Genetics and Human Health and the University of Wales, Aberystwyth. It was during these studies on a module called ITQB that he was first introduced to web development.

Chris progressed to a Consultant Role specialising in the recruitment of IT Developer’s throughout the UK. He went on to graduate from the University of Liverpool with a distinction in his Masters of Research degree in Post-Genomic Science. Here he developed his computer programming experience.

Chris has travelled extensively around Europe and has recently returned from volunteering as an English teacher in Vanuatu in the South Pacific at the Vanuautu's Little Star Summer School. Although he loves nothing more than exploring different places and cultures with his backpack, notebook and camera, Chester will always be his home.





Chris founded Everything Chester in June 2017 to showcase and develop his web development skills. He picked a topic close to his heart that he already knew lots about . . . Chester.
