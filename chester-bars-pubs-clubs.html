Clubs in Chester


The two main clubs in Chester are Rosies and Cruise.

RBs formally known as Rosies RBs is a Chester club that has three floors. There are different styles of music on each floor. The bottom floor plays 90s music, the middle floor is usually popular and dance music, while the top floor of Rosies varies hip-hop on a Sunday, rock and metal on a Wednesday and a mixture of hip-hop and dance and pop on other days.

Rosies Address: 12-16 Northgate Street, Chester, Cheshire, CH1 2HA

Cruise nightclub has six themed bar areas, including two dance areas.

Cruise Nightclub Address: 4 St Johns Street, Chester, CH1 1AT

Not quite clubs but bars that are also open late in Chester include Revolution, Missoula, Fiesta Hanvana, Bar Lounge and Watergates.
